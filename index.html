Twilight

Gender: Female (Ohio)

Birthday: April 5th

Traits: Kind, persistent, perseverance

Description: Twilight isn't your average Sprigatito. She was born with unusual powers most Sprigatitos didn't have. She hadn't known any of her family well because there was a fire in her old kingdom. The fire separated everyone, including Twilight. She fell in the water and washed up by the finish spot in the Celestial kingdom, meeting the Queen.

Motto: The gentle sweetheart who never gives up.

Twilight's Beginning

Twilight wasn't your average sprigatito. Born with powers others didn't have, she lost her family in a devastating fire that tore through her old kingdom. Swept away by water, she washed up in the Celestial kingdom, where she met the Queen and began a new chapter of her life.
